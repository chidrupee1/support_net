About Us

SUPPORT-NET is a digital platform dedicated to empowering rural school students by connecting them with the right support systems. Many students in rural areas face challenges such as lack of resources, guidance, or proper assistance. Our goal is to bridge this gap by providing a simple, secure, and effective way for students to express their concerns and receive help from the right individuals or organizations.
